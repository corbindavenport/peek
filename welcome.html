You may need to update permissions

Firefox may block Peek from running on a page until you click the Peek button in the toolbar. You can fix this by going to your add-ons list (about:addons) > Peek > Permissions tab > Access your data for all websites.

How to use Peek

Peek shows previews for documents, media files, social media posts, and other supported links. Just hover your mouse over a link to a file, and the preview will appear. Try the search results demo and hover over some results. The Peek settings allows you to change some options, like the viewer for documents.

Supported file types:

  • PDF, TXT, and RTF documents
  • WebM, MP4, M4V, OGG, OGV, and Imgur GIFV video
  • MP3, M4A, OGA, OGG, and WAV audio
  • Word documents (.doc, .docx)
  • Excel spreadsheets (.xls, .xlsx)
  • PowerPoint files (.ppt, .pptx)
  • OpenDocument files (.odt, .ods, etc.)
  • JPG, PNG, APNG, SVG, GIF, ICO, and BMP images

Supported links:

  • Google Docs links
  • iCloud Keynote links
  • YouTube videos and YouTube Shorts
  • Reddit links
  • Imgur links
  • TikTok videos
